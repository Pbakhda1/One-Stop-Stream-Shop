Search once. Stream anywhere.

OSSS is a concept for a universal streaming hub that helps you search for shows and movies (like Suits) and instantly see where you can watch — across platforms.

Demo mode: results use a sample catalog. Later, you can connect to a real streaming API.

How OSSS works (MVP concept)

1) Search a title

Enter a show or movie name and let OSSS look it up.

2) See where to watch

OSSS shows platforms and direct links (when available).

3) Save to your watchlist

Keep everything organized across services in one place.

In a real app, this connects to an official content database / streaming availability API and shows accurate results by country/region.

Vision

OSSS becomes the “home screen” for streaming — search, discover, track, and launch content without jumping between apps.
