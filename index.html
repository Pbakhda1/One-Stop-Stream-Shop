How OSSS works (MVP concept)

1) Search a title

Enter a show or movie name and let OSSS look it up.

2) See where to watch

OSSS shows platforms and “Watch Now” buttons.

3) Launch safely

Streaming opens in official platforms for secure playback.

In a real OSSS app, the search connects to a streaming availability API and returns accurate results by country.

Vision

OSSS becomes the “home screen” for streaming — search, discover, track, and launch content without jumping between apps.
